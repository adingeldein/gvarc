VE Testing Service

Amateur radio licensing is conducted by volunteer examiners (VEs) who volunteer their time administering the federally mandated tests in an effort to help the amateur service grow and improve. The tests are given by a minimum of three qualified VEs who ensure all regulations are followed and standards are upheld.

GVARC is not currently conducting testing sessions. However, stay tuned to our News page for updates on future testing sessions.
