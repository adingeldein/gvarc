Nets and Other Regular Activity

GVARC Ragchew Net
The club conducts an informal ragchew net every Wednesday at 19:45 (local) on the 147.000 repeater. All licensed amateur radio operators are welcome and encouraged to participate.
146.52 Simplex
Many area hams monitor 146.52 around the clock and a local "unclub" known as the Unusual Suspects is very active on this frequency. Crank up the power, put the omni online, and drop your call!
50.072 Beacon
A beacon is operational on 50.072. The beacon is located at the KF5KOI station in EM00ta and transmits at 10, 1, and 0.1 power levels at 5 second intervals. Signal reports are appreciated. Check out the [spot map](http://www.beaconspot.eu/beaconcm.php?beaconcall=KF5KOI&bandmhz=50)
